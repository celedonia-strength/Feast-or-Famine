Book with Ronnie @ronniegtattoos

Ronnie Gonzalez is the owner and lead artist at Feast or Famine Tattoo in Olympia, Washington. With a tattooing career that began in 2007 in Colton, California, Ronnie brings nearly two decades of experience and a deep passion for the craft. Known for his award-winning work in realism and portraiture, his style blends technical precision with a strong artistic sensibility, earning him recognition and respect throughout the tattoo community.

Ronnie opened Feast or Famine Tattoo as a space where quality, professionalism, and creative integrity come first. Whether you're looking for a detailed portrait, custom realism, or a concept brought fully to life on skin, Ronnie approaches every piece with dedication and care.
