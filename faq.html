Yes! Free touch-ups are available within 2–3 months of your appointment, depending on the tattoo and how it heals. Some exceptions may apply (fingers, feet, etc.).

Your artist will walk you through aftercare in person, but here's the short version:

  • Remove the bandage as instructed
  • Wash gently with unscented soap and warm water
  • Pat with a dry towel and let air dry
  • Apply a thin layer of tattoo aftercare ointment (A&D, Hustle Butter, or unscented lotion — ask your artist)
  • Avoid soaking, sun exposure, and picking at the tattoo while it heals

Healing usually takes about 2–4 weeks, depending on size and location.
