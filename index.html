Welcome toFeast or Famine Tattoo

Our Story

At Feast or Famine Tattoo, we wear our culture with pride and put our hearts into every piece we create. Rooted in Hispanic heritage and driven by a love for the craft, our shop is a space where tradition, storytelling, and bold expression come together through ink.

Whether you're just passing by or have been planning your next piece for months, you're always welcome here. Walk-ins are not just accepted—they're encouraged. We keep our doors (and our books) open for anyone ready to get tattooed, no matter how big or small the idea.

From fine-line details to bold blackwork and custom pieces, our artists bring both skill and soul to the table. At Feast or Famine, it's about more than just tattoos—it's about building a connection, embracing our community, and turning your story into something permanent.

Our Mission

Our mission at Feast or Famine Tattoo is to create high-quality, custom tattoos in a space that feels like home—welcoming, inclusive, and rooted in cultural pride. We honor our Hispanic background, value every client who walks through the door, and stay true to the hustle of our craft.

Whether you come in with a full sleeve idea or just want something off the cuff, we're here to make sure you leave with art that means something. No egos. No pressure. Just real people doing solid work with passion and respect.
